I was quickly enthralled with how much power being able to program offers. When stuck on a problem or bug I can hop onto a programming forum and look at advice and solutions people from all corners of the world have contributed. I love that programming is so international and constantly evolving and providing new challenges. It allows me to be an artist, explorer, and life-long learner.

Apart from being a programmer I have worked all over the world in many positions. I have worked in agriculture, social work and education. I believe I am an expert communicator and very creative. In every position I have held, I have left my mark on the company or organization with my new ideas and creativity. I am a very self-motivated individual and I look forward to using my creative energy and hard work for your company.
